Profile

I've shipped commercial software adopted internationally, led cross-functional engineering teams, and published peer-reviewed graphics research.

03 Distributed · AIGC

Distributed avatar-generation pipeline

Genies · Architect

Re-architected a monolithic autorigger into a distributed, individually schedulable pipeline of Kafka workers on Kubernetes/EKS — coordinating CPU-bound Blender/USD stages with GPU ML inference (body / hand / face pose estimation and segmentation) through a shared S3 workflow. I also led the broader AIGC initiatives: procedural 3D generation with diffusion models, texture synthesis, and the analytics platform that watches it all.

04 Rendering · Medical

Sub-micron dental rendering kernel

E4D Technologies · 3D Graphics Engineer

Engineered geometric kernels for topological transformations and high-order curve interpolation, hitting sub-micron accuracy in dental-margin modeling, plus a custom PBR scene-graph architecture that lifted rendering throughput by an order of magnitude. Shipped in E4D's commercial suite and integrated with Planmeca 3D imaging devices — adopted by clinics, labs, and teaching institutions across North America and Europe.
